About

After a hiatus from the kitchen for many years Grant has returned to the kitchen with a perfected recipe using premium ingredients and a traditional crust to keep you coming back for more!

After working in a 'mom and pop' pizza restaurant during his teenage years, Grant travelled the world for inspiration and returned with a new found passion to introduce new tastes to the Alberta diner.

His ability to deliver tantalizing flavour combinations utilizing a world culinary platter leaves you with a fulfulling dining experience
